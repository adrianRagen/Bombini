Eat


Bombini serves modern Italian food with a Japanese twist. Focused on the seasonality of the produce but more importantly on the flavour.


We offer a full vegetarian menu and we can accommodate most dietary requirements if mentioned during the reservation process for the standard tasting. At this stage we are unable to cater for Vegans.


Our Tasting Menu consists in a selection of 10 courses for $195pp. Our Premium Menu is now available for $275 and it consists in a 14 course degustation that includes premium ingredients like caviar or truffle.
